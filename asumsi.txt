Asumsi
1. Person's parents properties (fatherId and motherId) refer to biological parents
2. A person can only marry exactly one person at the same time
3. A person can have more than one former spouse
4. A person is divorced if and only if he/she has ended the last marriage
4.a. If a person was divorced in the past but now is married, isDivorced() will return false
5. getSiblings() consists biological brothers & sisters, and half brothers & sisters
6. getBrothers() consists biological and half brothers
7. getSister() consists biological and half sisters
